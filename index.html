LinkedIn Job Search URL Builder

Build custom job search URLs with bulk company filters. Skip the tedious one-by-one dropdown.

🔒 100% private — this tool runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
How it works
  1. Set your search filters below — keywords, location, job type, and more
  2. Add target companies by pasting their LinkedIn numeric IDs (bulk or one at a time)
  3. Copy the generated URL or open it directly in LinkedIn
✓ Your settings and company list auto-save in your browser — they'll be here when you come back.

Option A — From the company filter (fastest):

1. Go to LinkedIn Jobs and type a company name into the Company filter dropdown

2. Select it, then look at the URL — copy the number after f_C=

3. Paste it here. Repeat for each company.

Option B — From the company page:

1. Visit the company's LinkedIn page

2. Click "See all jobs" or "See all employees"

3. The ID appears in the URL after f_C= or currentCompany=

Option C — Direct URL check:

Visit linkedin.com/company/[ID] to verify any numeric ID — it redirects to the company page.
